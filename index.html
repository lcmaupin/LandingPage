Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned veteran . . .

Encabulation has never been easier.

At Brimplo, we believe encabulation shouldn't be tedious, messy, or expensive

Work Smarter! Our encabulators are designed with you in mind. Unlike older encabulators that required frequent tuning of the marzlevanes on the panametric fan, Brimplo encabulators are both self-adjusting and self-lubricating — no cumbersome tuning required! This increases uptime and minimizes the frustration caused by traditional encabulators. We’re proud of the performance of our encabulators and know you’ll have a great, seamless experience, but if you ever have an issue you can rest assured knowing we offer 100% lifetime support for our products, no questions asked

Go green! Brimplo is committed to carbon-neutrality and environmental responsibility. Conventional, old-fashioned encabulators rely on highly-radioactive amulite and carbon-intensive logarithmic shells. Global production of traditional encabulators produces as much atmospheric radiation as ten thousand Chernobyls and as many greenhouse emissions as the nation of Indonesia each year. Our encabulators are different. By employing our proprietary hemp-based Amurite and cold-forged algorithmic shells, we have reduced the environmental impact of encabulator production by 99.7%, so you can feel good knowing that your encabulator is as friendly to nature as it is to use!

Networking Brimplo encabulators is a breeze! All Brimplo encabulators are designed for easy network integration, right out of the box. Whether you’re linking your turboencabulator into an existing industrial or corporate network or using your microencabulator as the base machine for you Internet of Things smart home setup, you can feel confident that your Brimplo encabulator is built from the ground up to meet your needs. It doesn't matter if you’re incorporating a turboencabulator into your pre-existing production environment or just enjoying your first encabulation experience, our products are designed to give you a smooth and seamless experience.
